Our Story

We're more than just a donut shop - we're a Texan tradition.

From Our Family To Yours

Founded in 2010 by Lee family patriarch, our little donut shop started with a simple mission: to bring joy through freshly-made, delicious treats.

What began as a small shop with just six donut varieties has grown into a beloved Austin institution offering an extensive menu of artisanal donuts, specialty cakes, and hearty breakfast options.

We pride ourselves on using only the finest ingredients, with no preservatives or artificial flavors. Every donut, cake, and breakfast item is made fresh daily, using time-honored recipes with a modern twist.
